i only come here when i'm sad

I really wanted to do well. I put in so much effort. I didn't rest, I didn't sleep. Rather than the grade, I think it's just disappointing that scores don't always correlate to effort. The only upside I guess, is that this is a good way to stay grounded and realize how much left there is to grow and learn.

These moments occasionally make me reconsider whether I actually can eventually make an impact on the world. What legacy I will leave behind.

One way to really discover a person's nature is to see how the individual is not in his high but low points. I'm just a whiner but I don't mind crying over spilled milk.

Rant's over, I can't look at this anymore.
